FXbuddy Mascot — Mobile Variations

Five looping animations designed for phones (no mouse to track). Pick one for the mobile plugin hero.

A · Curious Wander

Eyes sweep to each corner on a slow loop, blinks occasionally. Approachable.

B · Excited Bounce

Hops up and down with a head-tilt, slight squash + stretch. High energy.

C · Breathing Zen

Gentle breathing scale with slow, sleepy blinks. Calm and premium.

D · Dancing Sway

Rhythmic tilt left-right while eyes dart with the beat. Playful, music-video friendly.

Lean-forward head tilt with eyes scanning around at random. Occasional "aha!" pop.
