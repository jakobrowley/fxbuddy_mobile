Hero Headline — 5 Variations

Tap Replay on any card to re-run that animation. Same copy, font, size, and spacing as the live mobile hero.

A · Word mask slide-up

Each word sits in an overflow-hidden mask and slides up from translateY(110%). Clean, no fade, no blur.

B · Letter scale-pop

Each letter punches in from scale(0.3) with a back-ease overshoot. Fast, snappy, playful.

C · Word 3D flip

Each word rotates from rotateX(-85°) to flat on a bottom hinge. Physical weight.

D · Words from alternating sides

Odd words arrive from the left, even words from the right. Stitched-together feel.

E · Line-by-line clip wipe

Whole lines wipe left-to-right via animated clip-path. Cinematic, title-card feel.
